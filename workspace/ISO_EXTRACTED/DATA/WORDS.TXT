Humans are . . . (description)
The Elves of Tunaria are intelligent, wise and charismatic beings that hold the delicate balance of the natural world as sacred above all things.
The Dark Elves are unrivaled in their cunning, wit, and malevolent drive to fulfill their unquenchable desire for personal power.
The Gnomes of Klick'Anon are a carefree race.  They are unrivaled masters of ingenious design and creativity.
Dwarves are a stout and hardy race whose temperament is jovial, though stubborn and unforgiving when crossed.
Trolls are vile creatures ruled by feral and vicious instinct, making them the wholly unpredictable and savagely cruel predators of Norrath's swamps.
Barbarians are the hardy stock of Northmen from which all humans originated.  They are the fierce, steely warriors of legend.
Agile and light of foot, Halflings are a friendly, jovial race that has an oftentimes-dangerous thirst for mischief and adventure.
Erudites, otherwise known as the High Men, are unrivaled in their affinity and mastery of the arcane sciences.
Ogres are a large and powerful race.  Aggressive and warlike, they never hesitate to attack those who stand against them.

Warriors are highly trained in armed combat.  They are most often found leading the charge in a headlong assault of their enemies.
These soldiers of the wilderness are unique in their abilities with the bow and unrivaled in their keen perception.  They also wield natural magics similar to that of the Druids.
Paladins are holy knights that fervently bear the most pure of righteous virtues and crusade to vanquish the corrupting darkness from Norrath.
These dark, callous, and malevolent knights passionately wield the venomous blade of corruption in service to the most cruel and wicked of the gods.
Monks are dedicated to the discipline and unification of mind, body and spirit; effectively turning their bodies into lethal weapons.
Bards are traveling musicians and scholars who seek the legends, secrets, and mysteries of the world.
Rogues live in the shadows, using cunning and stealth to accomplish their goals.
Druids derive their magic from the forces of nature and have devoted their very lives to protecting it.
Similar to a tribal witch doctor, shamans are usually found with the primitive races. They are connected to, and derive their power from, the unseen spirits that reside within all of Norrath.
These servants of the divine bear within them the power of their deity and often serve as healers and protectors by channeling the powers bestowed by their faith.
From the four primal elements to the creatures of Norrath, these masters of conjuration are peerless in their abilities to summon both servants and allies.
Necromancers are the masters of life force, controlling and fueling the essences of the undead with the corruption of the living.
Scholars of magic, enchanters focus on imbuing both objects and creatures with arcane properties. They are also adept at imposing the will of their minds on others.
Wizards are arcane masters unrivaled in their affinity for evocation and other realms of offensive magics.
Alchemists are an eccentric and inventive lot.  They emerge from their laboratories with powerful and explosive potions.
